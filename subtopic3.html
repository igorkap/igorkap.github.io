Providing Schools with Computers and Technology



URL
 Computers for Learning
Site Author
 USA Government
Abstract
 The Computers for Learning program website is designed to give computers to schools in need of technology in education.  It explains the rules, regulations, and eligibility requirements for the CFL program.  The CFL program is a non-profit organization, and only gives computers to schools promising to use them for the sole purpose of education.  It lists the countries that are eligible to participate and gives useful links to the frequently asked questions regarding the program, the types of educational institutions that participate, and also how to choose the equipment to meet that particular school's needs. 



URL
 PC's for Schools
Site Author
 The Computers for Schools Association, Copyrighted 2004
Abstract
 This is also another site designed to give computers to schools who are in need of them.  This website, however, is much different from the other one because it is not designed only for educational institutions.  It is also for regular people who want to help donate computers for education.  It gives links for people who wish to donate their old computers to students in need, and also for students and schools wishing to recieve a donated computer.  It also provides information for why it is necessary to have technology in education.  It talks about curriculum enhancement for students and the useful tools for teaching.  It also provides links to free software programs such as the Ziff-Davis Software Library which offers thousands of free downloads of popular programs.
